Our Story

Style Spaces was born from a singular vision: to create environments that transcend the ordinary and embrace the extraordinary. Founded in 2015, our journey began as a boutique design studio with an unwavering commitment to crafting spaces that tell meaningful stories.

“I've always believed that design is a profound conversation between past and present, within heritage and innovation.”

Manisha Shakya

Founder & Creative Director

“In my journey through design and life, I've discovered that the most beautiful spaces are those that evolve naturally from the way people live.”

Leela Joshi

Chief Executive Officer

Company Vision

Over the years, Style Spaces has evolved into a globally recognized design firm, orchestrating transformative projects across residential estates, boutique hotels, and prestigious commercial spaces. Our portfolio spans continents, yet our approach remains intensely personal. We believe that every environment we create should be as unique as the individuals who inhabit it. Our work has been recognized with numerous accolades, including multiple International Property Awards and the coveted Design Excellence Award. But our true measure of success lies in the lasting relationships we build with our clients and the timeless environments we create together.
